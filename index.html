Banana Catch

Move the basket to catch the falling bananas. You have 5 lives, and each life absorbs 10 missed bananas — so 50 misses and it's game over. Bananas speed up as you catch more!

🍌 Yellow = 1   🟢 Green = 2   🔴 Red = 3
🔵 Blue = 4   ✨ Golden = 100!   🌈 Rainbow = 2000!!
Rarer colors are worth more — the rainbow is legendary!
🤢 Rotten = avoid it! catching one slows your basket for 5s
🦅 Falcon = catch it for a 10s shield that blocks rotten bananas

Move with mouse, touch, or ← → arrow keys
